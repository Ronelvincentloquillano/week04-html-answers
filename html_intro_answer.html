Inception

A Sci-Fi thriller directed by Christopher Nolan.

Main Characters

Dom Cobb

A skilled thief who specializes in extracting secrets from people's dreams. He is haunted by his past and seeks a way to reunite with his children.

Arthur

Cobb's trusted partner and point man, responsible for research and keeping the team on track.

Ariadne

A brilliant young architect who helps design the complex dreamscapes for their heist.

Plot Summary

Inception follows Dom Cobb and his team as they attempt an impossible heist: planting an idea into a target's subconscious. To succeed, they must navigate multiple layers of dreams while battling Cobb's haunting memories.

Setting

Dream Layers

A series of surreal dreamscapes that become increasingly unstable as the team descends deeper into the subconscious.

Reality vs. Dream

The film blurs the line between reality and dreams, leaving the audience questioning what is real.
